About

Trolls!!

Troll folklore has facsinated me since I was a little girl. My parents got me a book about troll for xmas and inside there were the most beautiful images of trolls. Ive delved into every story i could that contained troll from old norse myths and folklore to the more modern day iterations. Films, television, books, songs, if it contained a troll I was in...

I hope you liked taking a look around this website, it's not finished, not by a mile. It only contains the smallest of snippets into the troll world, and would take a long time to properly complie all information on trolls. there is so much more to explore, there's Danish folklore, Greenland, various interperetations from other northern European countries.

We have to look more deeply at the troll tales which can range from funny and silly to downright terrifiying.
