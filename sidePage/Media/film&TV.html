Troll hunter

There are a variety of differing interpretaions on trolls across media. Some are large and hairy others are small almost human like.

Here we will look at a few of the different types a little more closely.

TrollHunter is the epitamy of troll movies, it covers everything troll. if there was a legitimate documentary on trolls, this would be it.

Each troll is crafted with such detail you would swear they did exist and these were the real thing. There is a family of snuffling cave trolls, a troll living under a bridge, a 3 headed troll that goes through the petrification process. There is even a large mountain troll with feet as large as a double decker bus.

This film covers everthing from trolls territory, to christian blood with lots inbetween. This is an absolute must watch for the troll enthusiast.

David and the Trolls

I watched david and the troll growing up. the troll description are near spot on and why wouldn't they be, this is a tale straight from the land of trolls itself.

The Trolls are micheivous tricksters, trying to get their hands on david and his family of gnomes. They are described as large, ugly, with bulbous noses, lynx like ears and tales with fur tufts on the end. a near exact description.

Watching David and the trolls is like watching the stories of trolls come to life.

Minus the brutality and man flesh eating.

The Hobbit

The Hobbit does so many things right and the depiction of trolls is no different. You could be forgiven if you thought the trolls in the hobbit, Bill(william, Tom and Bert were from an old norse tale.

Tolkien's description gives a the reader a great insight into the lives of Trolls. Dimm-witted, ugly, quick to anger, hungry for meat (of any kind) easily fooled and turn to stone in the sunlight.

Tolkiens description is the chef's kiss of troll descriptions. The film depiction of the trolls on the other hand, while great and closely matching tolkiens, gives the trolls a more ogreish look. Which is fine I suppose, their skins looks like cracking stone, their necks are short and their heads appear like boulders on the top.

They still want to eat the dwarves they've caught after killing them in horrible ways,

Troll in Central Park

This is a fun movie i think by disney, id not by someone trying to copy disney. It is a cute tale of a troll that wants to give up his troll ways and grow beautiful flowers.

The main Troll looks very similar to the more modern interperitation of troll, bright, large nose and large ears. Though he is not as hairy or ugly as his troll relative, who appear to be described more in the traditional way.

All in all Troll in central park is nice film with good characters and a great introduction for children into the world of trolls.
