What is a Troll

This is a blog all about trolls, big troll little troll, scary trolls and the inbetween.

Feel free to take a look around, we have a few famous troll tales, the mths of the lands they come from and if you like what you see, you can give us some feedback or take a quiz on what you've learned (located on the sidebar).

Take a look at the side links for some interesting, and our most popular posts.

There are external links in the footer for extended reading, and a quick email link to get in touch if you'd like. Or you can scan below for anything that takes your fancy.

All in all, I wish you a pleasant stay and I hope you enjoy your time here.
